Popular museums in Mexico

Frida Kahlo Museum

  • The Frida Kahlo Museum is a popular destination for both art lovers and those who want to learn more about the life and work of this iconic Mexican artist. It allows you to immerse yourself in the life and creative spirit of Frida Kahlo as you explore her home and her artistic legacy.

Anthropology National Museum

  • The National Museum of Anthropology is a cultural treasure that allows visitors to explore Mexico's rich history, its indigenous cultures and its artistic heritage. It is an essential place to understand the cultural diversity and archaeological wealth of Mexico and has played a fundamental role in the preservation and promotion of the nation's cultural heritage.

Soumaya Museum

  • The Soumaya Museum is a place that combines architectural beauty with a vast collection of art of high cultural and historical value. It is a cultural space that promotes free access to culture and enriches the cultural life of Mexico City. Its artistic diversity and architecture make it a must-see for art lovers and tourists.
